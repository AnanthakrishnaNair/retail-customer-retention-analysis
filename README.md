# Adidas Retail Customer Retention Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Customer Retention Analytics Dashboard developed in **Power BI** for Adidas. The objective was to analyze customer behavior, loyalty engagement, churn patterns, purchasing trends, and store performance to generate actionable business insights that improve customer retention and long-term profitability.

The dashboard consolidates multiple datasets into a single analytical solution, enabling business users to monitor customer performance, identify churn risks, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Customer retention is one of Adidas' biggest business challenges. Although large amounts of customer, transaction, loyalty, and store data are collected, they are spread across different sources, making it difficult to identify why customers leave and which customers should be prioritized for retention.

This project transforms raw business data into an interactive dashboard that answers critical retention and marketing questions.

---

# Project Objectives

* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for customer retention analysis.
* Analyze customer churn across different customer segments.
* Evaluate customer lifetime value (CLV).
* Study loyalty program effectiveness.
* Compare store and channel performance.
* Identify high-value and at-risk customers.
* Generate business recommendations to improve retention.

# Key Insights

* Customer inactivity is one of the strongest indicators of churn.
* High-value customers can still become inactive without targeted engagement.
* Loyalty participation alone does not guarantee retention.
* Store performance varies significantly across channels and regions.
* Improving loyalty engagement and personalized retention strategies can increase long-term customer value.

---

# Business Recommendations

* Prioritize retention of high-value customers through personalized campaigns.
* Improve engagement in underperforming channels and regions.
* Enhance the loyalty program with more attractive rewards and personalized offers.
* Identify at-risk customers earlier using customer activity indicators.
* Increase customer engagement through targeted retention campaigns instead of relying only on customer acquisition.
